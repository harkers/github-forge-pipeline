## Initiative Selection Rules

1. **One initiative per item** — Don't split work across multiple initiatives
2. **Use approved names only** — No unofficial abbreviations
3. **Inbox is temporary** — Move items out of Inbox during triage
4. **Comparable workstreams aligned** — ProPharma and HSBC use same structure

## Initiative vs Type

- **Initiative** = Where the work lives (bucket)
- **Type** = What kind of work it is (label)

Example:
- Initiative: Incidents
- Type: incident
- Workstream: Forge-ProPharma
